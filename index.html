About Mindy

I’m Mindy, an architect and writer from sunny Los Angeles, California, and I’ve taught English online for over 6 years, giving thousands of classes to hundreds of students from more than fifty-five countries.

I’m passionate about language learning, and I’ve created Wandering Word to share the English language with learners across the globe. We’re building a community dedicated to leveling up your English skills and self-confidence.

I’ve traveled to 42 countries and love meeting people from around the world. I’ll be your coach, teacher, guide, and friend as we explore all the quirky nuances and beautiful expressions that English has to offer!
